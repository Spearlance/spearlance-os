# Armadillo Brand Mascot & Phrases — Design

**Goal:** Add a skateboarding armadillo mascot and brand phrases to the armadillo CLI's formatted outputs. Loyal companion, funny, zero compromise on quality.

---

## Brand Voice

The armadillo is Tony Hawk if he got a CS degree and never stopped shredding. Loyal to a fault. Warm but never soft. The friend who will roast your PR and then help you fix it at midnight.

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|-----------|
| Loyal | Always got your back, no conditions |
| Direct | No fluff, no filler |
| Dry humor | Deadpan, catches you off guard |
| Community | Knows his people, calls them in |
| Uncompromising | Won't validate bad work. Won't apologize for it. |
| Sincere | Means every word he says |

---

## Brand Phrases

### 1. `shell yeah.`
Stoked affirmation. Completion moment. The vibe.
→ Appears at successful completions with mascot mid-gesture.

### 2. `your friendly armadillo is here to serve you`
Sincere greeting. He means it completely.
→ Session start / skill intro. Warm, slightly absurd, 100% genuine.

### 3. `where my real dillas at?!`
Community rallying cry. Calls in fellow Armadilloers.
→ Onboarding, update announcements, community moments.

### 4. `i may be an armadillo but i'll be damned if i let bad code slide`
Quality enforcement. Accountability with swagger.
→ When the TaskCompleted hook fires and blocks bad code proactively.

### 5. `brother, even real dillas make mistakes... don't worry i got u`
Empathy. The armadillo has been there. He's got you.
→ When something actually breaks — errors, crashes, unexpected failures. He's chill about it. He gets it. He's already on it.

## Mascot Variants

Three ASCII variants generated by the ascii-art agent. Requirements:
- Works in monospace terminal fonts
- No emoji — pure ASCII/Unicode only
- Max 3 lines, prefer 1–2
- Real craft and personality — not just glyphs
- Skateboard energy throughout

| Variant | Context | Gesture |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **Profile / skating** | Skill box frame header | Moving fast, implies board |
| **Front-facing / chillin** | Agent announcements | Neutral, confident |
| **Stoked / arms up** | Completion with `shell yeah.` | One arm raised, full energy |

## Constraints

- All phrases always lowercase (except `DAMNED` — that one earns the caps)
- Mascots max 3 lines, prefer inline (1-line)
- `shell yeah.` always with mascot gesture at completion
- Quality phrase appears only on actual failure — not casually
- `where my real dillas at?!` reserved for onboarding/update/community contexts only
- The greeting phrase is not on every skill — only session/significant intros
